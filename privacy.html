Privacy Policy

ComBoard

Last updated: June 21, 2026

ComBoard is an augmentative and alternative communication (AAC) app that helps you build and speak messages. We designed ComBoard to be private by default.

The short version: ComBoard does not collect, sell, or transmit your personal data. No accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no third‑party trackers. Everything the app needs happens on your device.

Information we collect

None that leaves your device. ComBoard has no servers of its own, no user accounts, and no analytics or advertising SDKs. We do not see, store, or receive anything you type, say, or do in the app.

What is processed on your device

Your messages and word learning. The words and phrases you use are stored only on your device so ComBoard can offer better predictions over time. This never leaves your device and is removed if you delete the app.

Text‑to‑speech. When you tap Speak, your text is read aloud using your device's built‑in speech voices. Your text is not sent to ComBoard or any third party for this.

Speech‑to‑text (the "Listen" button). When you choose to dictate, ComBoard uses your microphone and Apple's Speech Recognition framework to turn speech into text. ComBoard prefers on‑device recognition, so on supported devices your audio is transcribed entirely on the device and never transmitted. On some older devices or languages where on‑device recognition is unavailable, Apple's Speech framework may process the audio on Apple's servers under Apple's own privacy terms. ComBoard itself does not store your audio or send it anywhere, and recognition only runs while you are actively dictating.

Eye‑gaze control (optional). If you enable Eye Gaze Control, ComBoard uses the front camera and Apple's ARKit face tracking to estimate where you are looking so you can select keys hands‑free. The camera feed is analyzed in real time on your device to compute a gaze point. ComBoard does not record, save, photograph, or transmit any camera images or video. Tracking only runs while the feature is enabled.

Device permissions and why we ask

Microphone and Speech Recognition — only while you are dictating with the Listen button. Camera — only while Eye Gaze Control is enabled, to estimate gaze. No images are captured or stored. You can decline or revoke any of these in your device Settings; the rest of the app continues to work.

Third parties

ComBoard uses Apple's built‑in system frameworks (speech synthesis, speech recognition, ARKit), which operate under Apple's privacy terms. ComBoard integrates no third‑party analytics, advertising, or tracking, and shares data with no one.

Children

ComBoard is used by people of all ages, including children. Because the app collects no personal data and transmits nothing, it does not gather information from children or anyone else.

Data retention and deletion

All app data (your learned words, settings) lives on your device. Deleting the app removes it. We hold nothing to delete on our side because we never receive your data.

Changes to this policy

If we add features that change how data is handled, we will update this policy and the date above before that feature ships.
